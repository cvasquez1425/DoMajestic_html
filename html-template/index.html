ABOUT US

Our interesting story

Johanna has been with Salon A for over 16 years. She specializes in coloring, custom blending, highlights, lowlights, and color corrections using L'Oreal Professionel Coloring and Scruples Blazing highlighting system. Johanna is also certified in the Brazilian Blowout smoothing treatment, an amazing treatment that smoothes and defrizzes hair, leaving it silky and shiny.

Mayrelis mastered her haircutting skills through extended training at Paul Mitchell, Toni & Guy, Rusk, and ICON academies. Her devotion to her clients over the years has allowed her to develop a special bond with each and everyone. Mayrelis has been such a devoted and outstanding stylist and team player that she has earned the position of "lead stylist".

To further Johanna and Mayrelis artistic techniques in color, cutting and styling and to discover new products, they travels regularly to fashion centers in major U.S. cities such as New York, Orlando, Las Vegas, Miami and California. They also attends international beauty shows. Most recently, the statewide fashion publication in Orlando, Brilliant Magazine.
